Subscribe leverage token ticker stream

**Push frequency:** 300ms

**Topic:**<br/>
`tickers_lt.{symbol}` e.g.,tickers_lt.BTC3SUSDT [确认symbol的写法，是BTC3S还是BTC3SUSDT]

### Response Parameters
| Parameter | Type | Comments|
|:----- |:-----|----- |
|topic |string |Topic name |
|type |string |Data type. `snapshot` |
|ts |integer |The timestamp (ms) that system generates the data |
|data |array |Object |
|> symbol |string |Symbol name |
|> price24hPcnt |string |Market price change percentage in the past 24 hours |
|> lastPrice |string |The last price |
|> prevPrice24h |string |Market price 24 hours ago |
|> highPrice24h |array |Highest price in the past 24 hours  |
|> lowPrice24h |string |Lowest price in the past 24 hours |
