> cover: USDT Perpetual, USDC Perpetual, Inverse Perpetual, Inverse Future, Option

Get latest information of the symbol

:::info important
* Future has `snapshot` and `delta` types. If a key does not exist in the field, it means the value is not changed.
* Option has `snapshot` data ONLY.
:::

Push frequency: **real-time**

**Topic:**
`tickers.{symbol}`

### Response Parameters
| Parameter | Type | Comments|
|:----- |:-----|----- |
|id |string |Message id. _Unique field for option_ |
|topic |string |Topic name |
|type |string |Message type. `snaptshot`,`delta`|
|ts |number |The timestamp (ms) that system generates the data. |
|cs |number |Please ignore |
|data |Object | |
|> symbol |string |Symbol name |
|> [tickDirection](../enum#tickdirection) |string |Direction of price change. _Unique for future_ |
|> lastPrice |string |Last price |
|> bid1Price |string |Bid 1 price. _Unique for future_ |
|> bid1Size |string |Bid 1 size. _Unique for future_ |
|> ask1Price |string |Ask 1 price. _Unique for future_ |
|> ask1Size |string |Ask 1 size. _Unique for future_ |
|> price24hPcnt |string |Percentage change of market price relative to 24h. _Unique for future_ |
|> prevPrice24h |string |Price of 24 hours ago. _Unique for future_ |
|> highPrice24h |string |The highest price in the last 24 hours |
|> lowPrice24h |string |The lowest price in the last 24 hours |
|> prevPrice1h |string |Hourly market price an hour ago |
|> change24h |string |24-hour change. _Unique for option_ |
|> markPrice |string |Mark price |
|> indexPrice |string |Index price |
|> openInterest |string |Open interest |
|> turnover24h |string |24h-turnover |
|> volume24h |string |24h-volume |
|> nextFundingTime |string |Next funding time. _Unique for future_ |
|> fundingRate |string |Funding rate. _Unique for future_ |
|> predictedFundingRate |string |Predicated funding rate. _Unique for future_ |
|> bidPrice |string |Bid 1 price. _Unique for option_ |
|> bidSize |string |Bid 1 size. _Unique for option_ |
|> bidIv |string |Bid iv. _Unique for option_ |
|> askPrice |string |Ask 1 price. _Unique for option_ |
|> askSize |string |Ask 1 size. _Unique for option_ |
|> askIv |string |Ask iv. _Unique for option_ |
|> markPriceIv |string |Mark pruce iv. _Unique for option_ |
|> underlyingPrice |string |Underlying price. _Unique for option_ |
|> totalVolume |string |Total volume. _Unique for option_ |
|> totalTurnover |string |Total turnover. _Unique for option_ |
|> delta |string |delta value. _Unique for option_ |
|> gamma |string |gamma value. _Unique for option_ |
|> vega |string |vega value. _Unique for option_ |
|> theta |string |theta value. _Unique for option_ |
|> predictedDeliveryPrice |string |Predicted delivery price. _Unique for option_ |
